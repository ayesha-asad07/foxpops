The Concept

The Problem
We Solve

Foxnuts (Makhana) are among the richest plant-based sources of calcium — yet they remain overlooked in Pakistan's mainstream snack market. Plain foxnuts fail to compete with intensely flavored chips and crackers.

FoxPops bridges this gap by reimagining the foxnut with bold, modern flavors and clear nutritional messaging — giving health-conscious snackers a reason to make the smart choice.

01
Lack of Flavor Variety
Plain foxnuts fail to compete in a market dominated by intensely flavored chips and snacks. Consumer attention demands sensory appeal.
02
Low Consumer Awareness
A significant portion of the population is unaware of foxnuts' exceptional calcium content — especially critical for young adults, women, and the elderly.
03
Our Answer
Bold flavor coatings + resealable packaging + prominent nutritional labeling = a snack that wins on taste and health simultaneously.
